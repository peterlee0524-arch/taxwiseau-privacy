Effective date: 24 May 2026

1. Overview

TaxWiseAU ("the App") is an on-device financial planning calculator for Australian residents. This policy explains how Wise Choice Australasia Pty Ltd ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the App.

Summary: TaxWiseAU collects no personal data, sends no data to any server, and requires no account or internet connection.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information.

All inputs you enter into the App (income, loan amounts, superannuation balance, asset purchase prices, etc.) exist only in your device's memory while the App is running. They are not written to disk beyond Apple's standard app-state preservation, and they are never transmitted off your device.

3. Analytics and Tracking

TaxWiseAU does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash-reporting services, or tracking frameworks. We do not use Apple's App Analytics unless you have opted in to sharing analytics with developers via your iOS Settings.

4. Internet Access

The App does not make any network requests. No internet permission is required or used. All tax calculations are performed entirely on-device using rules bundled with the App.

5. Children's Privacy

TaxWiseAU is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, advertising networks, or social media platforms. The only external service involved in distribution is Apple's App Store, which is governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy.

7. Data Retention

Because we do not collect any data, there is nothing to retain or delete. If you delete the App, all locally stored app preferences (such as your selected language) are removed from your device.

8. Your Rights

As we hold no personal data about you, there is no personal data to access, correct, or erase. If you have questions, you may contact us at the address below and we will respond within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when material changes occur, for example when a new feature is added that involves data processing. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. Continued use of TaxWiseAU after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
